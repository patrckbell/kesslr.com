About Us

Our Story

While working at Rocket Lab, we witnessed firsthand the challenges space companies face with outdated computing systems. We saw brilliant teams struggle with decades-old technology that couldn't keep up with modern demands.

We believed space technology should be as accessible and powerful as modern AI, not held back by systems designed in a different era. So we left successful careers to solve a problem we knew intimately from hundreds of test flights.

The Problem We Saw

Space companies were stuck choosing between reliable but outdated systems and powerful but unsuitable modern technology. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our Mission

"Making Space Easy" means democratizing access to advanced space technology. We're breaking down the technical and cost barriers that prevent innovation in space, giving small teams the tools that once required massive budgets.

We're empowering the next generation of space entrepreneurs to compete with established players, accelerating innovation and enabling dreams that were previously impossible.

The Founders

Our team brings deep experience from Rocket Lab, one of the most successful private space companies. We have direct knowledge from hundreds of actual space missions and understand both the technical challenges and business realities of space.

We're driven by passion for innovation and a desire to solve problems we lived through firsthand. Our unique position allows us to bridge the gap between heritage systems and modern AI.

Why We Started

We were frustrated watching brilliant space companies struggle with outdated tools. We believed modern computing could work in space if designed properly, and we were in a unique position to make it happen.

Our Values

Mission-First

Every decision is driven by what's best for space missions and the people who depend on them.

Accessibility

Making advanced technology available to everyone, not just the industry giants.

Reliability

Building systems that work when failure isn't an option, every single time.

Innovation

Constantly pushing what's possible in space technology and never settling for the status quo.

The Impact We Want

We're leveling the playing field, allowing small companies to compete with established players. We're accelerating innovation with faster development cycles for space technology.

Most importantly, we're enabling dreams and supporting the next generation of space entrepreneurs who will advance humanity's expansion into space.
